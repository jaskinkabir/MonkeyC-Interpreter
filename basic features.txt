MonkeyC language

Features:
    Keywords: ooh, aah, hoo, haa, hooh, haah, ooohooh, woo, waa, eee 

    Comments:
        Begin comment line with '#', must end comment lines with ;

    Variable definition: Implicit types:
        var: hoo
        definition(=): ooh
         
    Basic syntax:
        print: wee
        if: ooaah
        else: waa
        for: hee
        input: eeeaah
    Builtins:
        length: haahoowaa
    
    Logical operators:
        ==: oohooh
        not: woa
        or: eee
        and: oohwee
        >: aah
        <: haa

    Numerical operators:
        +: eeeooh
        -: oohee
        /: haahee
        *: heehaa
        %: hoowaa
